Looking for Support?

We offer support for all of our themes, be it through comments on our items or via the wonderful medium of e-mails. Our support forums are currently being built and should be launched early in 2012.

Not only will the site be for support but it’ll be a great resource for everything WordPress – we’ll be giving away free themes, code snippets, design goodies and all sorts of other wonderful thingermejigs.

If you’ve got any suggestions, questions or other things that end in ‘ions’ for us then feel free to use the contact form below and we’ll do our very best to get back to you as soon as we can.

No need for a contact form plugin?

No need for extra plugins. No need for faffing around with complicated form building. Activate theme, answer 3 questions, press save, Insert shortcode. Job. Done. Easy – just like it should be. Or if you already use another contact form, we wont get in your way!
 

Regularly updated and improved upon

We update our themes on a regular basis and often add great new features as we further develop our framework. In 2012, we’ll be releasing the framework as a free resource for other WordPress developers.
